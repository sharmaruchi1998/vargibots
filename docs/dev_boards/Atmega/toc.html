ATmega2560 Development Board

About the Development Board:

SmartElex ATMEGA2560 Development Board is a powerful development platform based on ATMEGA2560 microcontroller which is one of the most feature-rich AVR microcontrollers from Atmel, featuring 256K Flash, 8K RAM, 86 I/O lines arranged in nine 8 bit ports, 16 ADCs, 4 UARTs, 6 timers, 8 interrupts and much more. This board is ideal for developing embedded applications involving high-speed wireless communication, USB-based data logging, real-time data monitoring, and control etc.

  • Board Name: SmartElex ATmega2560 Development Board
  • Microcontroller: ATMEGA2560 with 16 MHz crystal with On-Board USBASP Programmer.
  • Power: 7 to 15 VDC with Reverse Polarity Protection.
  • 2.4GHz ZigBee (XBee) wireless module adaptor
  • 2×16 Character Alphanumeric LCD.
  • SD card Holder.
  • CH340G USB to Serial converter.
  • ESP8255 Adaptor interface with USB to Serial Converter.
  • Connector for 2 servo motors.
  • DS1307 based Real-Time Clock with Battery Backup.
  • ULN2003 7 channel 500mA driver.
  • L293D 600mA Dual DC motor Driver.
  • 1 Analog Potentiometers, 8 Switches, 8 LEDs, Buzzer.
  • Switches: Reset, Power (ON-OFF SWITCH).
  • Male Burg Pin Connectors for all ports.
